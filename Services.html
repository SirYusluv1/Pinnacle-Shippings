AIR FREIGHT SOLUTIONS

Airfreight is the transport mode of choice for priority deliveries. A common misconception is to associate airfreight with medium to small cargo. We can offer a Pinnacle Solution for almost in imaginable cargo: Up to 10m in diameter ; 70m length (Max payload = 249 000kgs).

RAIL-CARGO
SOLUTIONS

Recent multi-billion Rand investments by the South African government has seen remarkable upgrades to our national rail infrastructure. Rail is fast becoming the modality of choice for bulk / break bulk & containerized cargo. To explore the cost savings associated with rail, we are readily available to custom design a Pinnacle Solution, for your business.

ROAD FREIGHT SOLUTIONS

From consolidated cargo (1kg) to project work (100 000 kg), we can offer you a Pinnacle Solution, to any road freight challenge. We have the ability to deliver to all major Southern African destinations - if you're anywhere south of the equator, you're probably within our reach, by road.

OCEAN FREIGHT SOLUTIONS

Ocean Freight is the bedrock of International Trade, hence it is no co-incidence that the word "Shipping" is incorporated into our name. Consider what a Pinnacle Solution will deliver to your business. We offer both Port to Port & Door to Door services for all Containerized, Break Bulk or RoRo cargo.
