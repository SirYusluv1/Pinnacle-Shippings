THE PINNACLE WAY

Pinnacle Shippings Solutions commenced trading in June 2016, with a clearly defined mandate to provide a modern Solution, to global freight challenges.
We pay homage to Mamma Africa & endeavour to reflect her strength of character in our core values, as follows:



AFRICA

Logistically speaking, Africa is without doubt "the final frontier". Beset with challenges & yet laden with reward, for those who dare venture beyond their comfort zone.
No other place on earth does the phrase "Fortune favours the Brave" have more relevance.



FREIGHT

As with conventional thinking "no idea is too small or too large",
for us no cargo is too small or too large. You may be pleasantly surprised by our capacity to deliver
varying types of cargo, using conventional modes of transport (Air, Road, Rail & Sea).



RIGHTS

Pinnacle Shippings Solutions' clients are entitled to Pinnacle Service.
You're assured of (1) competitive value (2) honesty in all interactions
(3) insight & understanding the often confusing world of freight processes,
procedures & terminology (freight jargon & incoterms).



INTEGRITY

All relationships will be entered into on principals of good character, honour, ethics, sincerity & trustworthiness. 



CARE

Special care will be taken to ensure your cargo is delivered in the exact condition we received it in.
Our Pinnacle Inspection process far surpasses the industry norms & instils confidence that all cargo will be
delivered in the exact condition, as received. 



ACHIEVE

We shall endeavour to Attentively Acquire knowledge, Achieve Admirable Accomplishments, Astonish with Amazing results, thus Acquiring Abundant Acclaim.
